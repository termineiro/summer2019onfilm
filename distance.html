SUMMER 2019 CAPTURED ON FILM by Carlos Mineiro

This summer, I took an old camera, went on adventures, & captured some of it on film.

"DISTANCE"

As a shy photographer, in the beginning, all I wanted to do was capture the world around me. Buildings, skylines, streets, sunsets, alleyways. Anything that looked urban and interesting, I captured a shot, from a distance, not really interacting with my environment.
